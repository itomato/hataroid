About

Hataroid is an Android port by squalldc of the open source Atari ST/STE/Falcon emulator Hatari.

FAQ

What is needed to get this thing to run?
Hataroid requires at the bare minimum a TOS image to boot up the system.

Why are there two TOS image configuration settings?
One is to use when in ST mode and one to use in STE mode. This will enable you to easily switch machines without reconfiguring the TOS paths.

What does map arrow keys do?
The on-screen joystick directions will now map to the cursor keys on the keyboard.

What is mouse button emulation (direct) and how do I right click?
You can tap anywhere to trigger a left mouse button press. This is similar to using the trackpad on a laptop.
To trigger a right button click, you tap two fingers one after the other in quick succession (like on a popular brand of thin notebooks)

This mouse button emulation (direct) sucks. Can't I just have some buttons on the screen?
The on-screen buttons work better when playing games. You can change this in the input settings.

The on-screen controls are in the way. How do I hide them?
You can change the transparency in the input settings.
